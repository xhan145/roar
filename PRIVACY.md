# ROAR Privacy

ROAR is local-first.

- No cloud transcription.
- No telemetry.
- No account requirement.
- No subscription requirement.
- No automatic network calls at startup.
- The About screen's Check for updates action is the only intentional network action, and it runs only after the user clicks it.

Local data may include `config.json`, `history.db`, optional retained audio WAV files, local logs, snippets, vocabulary, and downloaded or bundled model files. Privacy controls, history deletion, and retained-audio deletion are Core behavior and must not require a paid license.

Safe Diagnostics intentionally exclude transcript text, raw clipboard contents, audio contents, snippets, vocabulary, full window titles, and license secrets.
