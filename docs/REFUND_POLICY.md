# Refund Policy (draft)

> **Draft — review with counsel before launch.**

If ROAR does not work on your machine, email support within **14 days** of
purchase for a refund.

## What this covers

- ROAR fails to run, dictate, or type on your supported Windows setup, and support
  cannot resolve it.

## How to request

Email **gregm@joincollective.net** with your order
reference and a short description of the problem. See [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md). We
do not need your transcripts or audio.

## Notes

- One-time purchase; no subscription to cancel.
- This is a starting policy to be finalized with legal review and your payment
  processor's requirements before a paid launch.
