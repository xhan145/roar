# FAQ

**Does ROAR work offline?**
Yes. Transcription runs entirely on your machine. ROAR does not need the internet
for normal use, and license validation is offline once your license is imported.

**Do I need an account?**
No. There is no account, no login, and no profile. You own a local license file.

**Is this a subscription?**
No subscription. ROAR is a one-time purchase — buy once, use forever, including
all v1.x updates.

**Where is my voice processed?**
On your computer, locally. Your audio and transcripts never leave your machine.
There is no cloud transcription.

**What does Core include?**
Push-to-talk and hands-free dictation, streaming preview, multilingual dictation,
history, every privacy and delete control, basic vocabulary, spoken commands, and
scratch-that — free, forever.

**What does Pro include?**
Everything in Core plus advanced milestones, smart formatting, snippet packs and
extended snippet variables, vocabulary suggestions, history filters/tags,
advanced cleanup controls, and settings import/export.

**What does Developer Pack include?**
Everything in Pro plus code mode, symbol dictation, app profiles, per-app
language, project vocabulary, file tagging, and developer snippet packs.

**What happens if I lose my license?**
Re-import your license file and you are back to your edition. Keep a copy with
your backups. If you cannot find it, contact support (see [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md)).

**Does ROAR collect telemetry?**
No. ROAR has no telemetry, analytics, or background network calls.

**Can I get a refund if it does not work?**
Yes — see [REFUND_POLICY.md](REFUND_POLICY.md). If ROAR does not work on your
machine, email support within 14 days.

**Are privacy controls paid?**
Never. Privacy controls, deleting history, and deleting audio are free in every
edition — including Core.
