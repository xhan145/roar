# Founder & Company Readiness

**This is a readiness checklist, not legal advice.** Have a qualified attorney and
CPA review ownership, entity choice, and tax before any paid launch.

## ⚠️ Launch gate

Do **not** take money until IP ownership and company control are clean. Selling
first and formalizing later creates disputes that are expensive to unwind.

## Founder split (placeholder)

Currently set to **50 / 25 / 25** — a placeholder to be confirmed in writing.

- [ ] Confirm the split with all founders in writing.
- [ ] **Vesting** on all founder equity (e.g., 4-year with a 1-year cliff) — required.
- [ ] **IP assignment** from every founder/contractor to the company — required.
- [ ] **Expense reimbursement policy** agreed and documented — required.

## Company-owned accounts (transfer off personal accounts)

- [ ] Source repository under company control.
- [ ] Domain(s) under company control.
- [ ] Payment processor account under company control.
- [ ] Support email + admin accounts under company control.
- [ ] Signing keys / secrets under company control (never in the repo).

## Entity

- [ ] Decide **LLC vs C-Corp** (tax treatment, investment plans, number of
      owners) — with a CPA/attorney.
- [ ] Form the entity.
- [ ] Founder agreement signed.
- [ ] IP assigned to the entity.
- [ ] Bank account opened; finances separated from personal.

## Review

- [ ] Attorney review of founder agreement, IP assignment, and terms.
- [ ] CPA review of entity choice and tax handling.

Nothing in this document is legal or tax advice.
