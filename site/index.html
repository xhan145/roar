The privacy promise

This isn't a marketing line — it's how the app is built.

  • Your voice never leaves your machine. Transcription runs locally. There is no cloud path to disable — there isn't one.
  • No telemetry, ever. ROAR only touches the network if you click "check for updates".
  • Your licence is checked offline. No account, no sign-in, no licence server phoning home.
  • Privacy is never paid. History and audio deletion, retention controls and offline use are free in every edition — permanently.

Questions

Is Core a trial?

No. Core is free dictation you can use forever — it doesn't expire, nag, or lock up. The paid editions add workflow power on top; they don't unlock the basics.

Do I need an account or an internet connection?

Neither. ROAR transcribes locally and verifies your licence on your own device. It works on a machine that has never been online.

Is this a subscription?

No. It's a one-time purchase. All v1.x updates are included. A future major version may be an optional paid upgrade, and the version you bought will keep working.

What happens to my dictation history?

It stays on your machine in a local database — or nowhere at all, if you turn it off. You can delete it, and any retained audio, at any time. Those controls are free in every edition.

What's the difference between Developer and Supporter?

Functionally, nothing. Supporter has exactly the same capabilities as Developer. It exists for people who want to fund independent development, and it puts a supporter badge on your licence screen.

Which GPUs are supported?

NVIDIA via CUDA out of the box. AMD and Intel GPUs are supported through an optional Vulkan engine you can enable in Settings. No GPU? A tuned CPU path is the default.
